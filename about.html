👤 About the Developer

Hey, I'm Michael — a student studying IT and web development, and a self-described newbie typist. I built TypeScripture because I got tired of typing random words and wanted my practice to actually mean something.

"Therefore being justified by faith, we have peace with God through our Lord Jesus Christ:" Romans 5:1

I was grinding away on typing games and it hit me — why am I memorizing nonsense when I could be learning scripture at the same time? So I decided to build it myself using AI.

📖 How it happened
✈️
The idea started on a trip to Korea. I went to meet my fiancée's family. While everyone was sleeping I opened Google Gemini and built a rough version overnight just to show her before she woke up.
📱
I "beta tested" it on the 16 hour flight. I propped my phone up against my laptop, opened my Bible app to Acts 7, and typed the whole thing into Notepad. That was the first real test.
✝️
I made this for God's glory, not for profit. If you sign up I'm not going to sell your email, spam you, or do anything weird with your data. It's just a typing app built by a guy who loves the Word.
🇰🇷
The first version is still live at michaelcordell95.github.io/bibletype — it even has a Korean mode I built so her family could use it.
